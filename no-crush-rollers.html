No Crush Rollers

We supply only the highest quality NO CRUSH ROLLERS for feed rolls, hold down rolls, and transfer sections.

Made of long lasting, specially formulated polyurethane, the Wagner Industries, Inc. line of no crush products is among the most extensive in the world.

AVAILABLE FOR ALL MANUFACTURERS.

We can offer a no crush roller for any machine or application.

We specialize in providing a variety of sizes and applications. Our comprehensive inventory of moulds and range of durometers makes us uniquely qualified to supply you with a no crush roller for any application.
